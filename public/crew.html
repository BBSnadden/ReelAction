Meet Our Crew

Dedicated professionals ensuring your fishing adventure is unforgettable.

Our Team

Mark Snadden

Role: Skipper Owner

Owner and Skipper, Mark Snadden, has been in the recreational, charter fishing, and boating arena for most of his life and is the longest-serving charter boat operator in South Australia – over 35 years. Mark is also an owner of Reel Action Fishing Charters in Marion Bay, which has won numerous game fishing tournaments, including being champion angler and champion boat. Mark is the voice on the phone and the man behind the emails. He primarily runs the charters out of Marion Bay.

Cody Snadden

Role: Crew

A young lad with a couple of records to his name, from time to time you’ll find Cody on the deck. He has quite a bit of experience, having been born with the fishing bug in his blood. Cody’s specialty is line class and has won numerous awards, including a World Record for a Samson fish on light line, and has participated in the 2018 & 2021 Tuna Shootout in Lincoln.

Jenny Snadden

Role: Deckie

Jenny is the better half to Mark and will run the deck better than most of the guys, so don’t get in her way :). She’s also been known to chase a record or two, with a 22.5kg Gummy on 6kg line delivering a State & Australian record. Jenny was also a Dive Instructor.

Danny

Role: Crew

Danny is a new addition to our team this year and has a number of years' experience fishing and with charters. You may have seen him with Mark out of Marion Bay as well as Glenelg. Danny enjoys being on the water, helping out on the deck, and is currently stepping up to be a Skipper.

Michael

Role: Crew

Having a love of fishing is what drew Michael to Glenelg Fishing Charters, and his knowledge is beyond his years. Michael will make you feel at ease on the deck, whether you are new to fishing or have cast a line or two.

Andy

Role: Crew

Andy started out as a regular customer of ours at Marion Bay and loved being on the water so much, he joined the team. Although he primarily accompanies Mark out of Marion Bay, you’ll also see Andy working the deck out of Glenelg.
