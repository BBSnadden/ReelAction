About Us


Reel Action Fishing Charters operates from Marion Bay, South Australia, led by owner and passionate fisherman, Mark, who has operated in these waters since 1987.
Specialising in small to medium group charters, ensuring personalised experiences. Our vessel, “Raptor,” is a fast and reliable boat, giving you more time fishing and less time traveling.
Whether you’re after Nannygai, Whiting, Sampson, Kingfish, or Tuna, our charters offer access to a wide array of fish species.
Reel Action is a full-time, professional charter service committed to top-notch customer service. Join us to experience the best fishing opportunities the lower Yorke Peninsula has to offer.
