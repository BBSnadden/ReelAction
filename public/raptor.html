About Raptor

Raptor is a 30-foot custom-built Kevlacat (2008), designed for offshore fishing adventures. This high-performance vessel is fully surveyed under 2B and 2C classifications, licensed to carry 12 passengers plus crew up to 50 nautical miles offshore.

  • Twin 250hp Yamaha four-stroke engines – cruise at 26 knots, top speed of 42 knots.
  • 900-litre fuel capacity – range of 300 nautical miles for long-range charters.
  • Furuno electronics package – includes radar, autopilot, GPS, and sounder.
  • Premium fishing equipment – “Relax” outriggers, “Top Shot” gaffs, tag poles, and high-end rods & reels.
  • Live bait tank & kill box – ensuring your catch stays in peak condition.
  • Over 9 square meters of deck space – plenty of room for battling big fish.
  • Fully equipped for multiple fishing styles – reef fishing, trolling, and live baiting.
  • Private dinette, toilet, and bunk beds – ensuring comfort for long trips.
